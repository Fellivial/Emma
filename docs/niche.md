# Emma's Niche

Emma is for emotionally underserved people who want an AI companion that feels genuinely present in their daily lives and currently use products like Replika or ChatGPT but are frustrated that those relationships have little memory, context, or ability to help with real-world tasks.

## What this means for product decisions

- Emma's moat is warmth + memory + real-world utility — in that order
- The persona (mommy, avatar, voice) is the differentiator, not the integrations
- Integrations (Gmail, Calendar, Slack, Notion) make the relationship _useful_, not productive-first
- Features that serve productivity without emotional presence are not Emma's wedge
- The target user is closer to a frustrated Replika user than a frustrated Notion AI user
